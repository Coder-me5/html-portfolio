The Best Movies According to Murli

My all time best three movies.

The Pursuit of Happyness.

This is the best movie very often I watch. It gives your vision a taste of life. The Pursuit of Happyness is a book and movie about a struggling salesman who overcomes adversity to provide for his son. The story is about resilience, determination, and the human spirit

Schindler's List.

This movie is my all time favourite movie. The Schindler's List is a historical drama film about a German industrialist who saved Jewish people from the Holocaust during World War II. The film is based on the true story of Oskar Schindler, a Czech-born businessman who saved over 1,100 Jews.

Kashmir Files.

The Kashmir Files is a 2022 Hindi-language Bollywood drama film that depicts the 1990 exodus of Kashmiri Hindus from Indian-administered Kashmir. The film is a fictionalized account of the events leading up to the exodus and the exodus itself
